Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our comprehensive interior design services, including space planning, furniture selection, lighting design, and custom cabinetry.

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of interior design services, including space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, and custom cabinetry. We also provide renovation services, project management, and 3D renderings to help visualize your space before making any changes.

How much do your interior design services cost?

The cost of our interior design services varies depending on the scope of the project, the size of the space, and your specific requirements. We offer both hourly consulting rates and flat fees for full design projects. After an initial consultation, we can provide a detailed quote tailored to your needs.

How do you work with clients during the design process?

We start with an initial consultation to understand your style, needs, and budget. From there, we create a design plan, including mood boards, layout options, and material selections. We collaborate closely with you throughout the process, ensuring your vision is brought to life. Regular updates and meetings keep you involved every step of the way.

Do you offer eco-friendly or sustainable design options?

Yes, we are committed to sustainable design practices. We offer eco-friendly options such as using non-toxic materials, energy-efficient lighting, and sustainably sourced furniture. We can tailor your design to include as many green elements as you desire.
